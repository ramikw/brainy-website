Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Brainy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with regulations like the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. What data do we collect?

We collect the following personal identification information that you provide directly:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (stored in a secure, hashed format)

Learning and Usage Data: Data generated from your activity within the App, such as your folder and files, reviews, and content within files.

2. How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with all of the data we collect when you:

  • Register for an account in the App.
  • Use the App. To maintain your progress across devices, data is automatically synced. You have full control over this feature and can disable automatic sync or trigger manual sync at any time.

3. How will we use your data?

We collect and process your data so that we can:

  • Manage your account and provide our service: To create your account and authenticate you.
  • Synchronize your data: To ensure your learning progress and data are consistent and available across all devices where you are logged in.
  • Send you emails, including transactional service communications.

Legal Basis for Processing (EU GDPR): We process your personal data on the basis of performing our contract with you (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) to provide the App's services. For any optional processing, we will ask for your clear consent.

4. How do we store your data?

We securely store your data on our servers. All data in transit is protected using TLS encryption.

Data Retention: We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time via the App's settings. Upon account deletion, we will promptly remove your personal data from our primary production systems. Please note that some data may persist in our encrypted backup systems for a limited period before being automatically overwritten, as part of our standard disaster recovery procedures.

Automatic Deletion Due to Inactivity: To minimize unnecessary data retention, accounts with no sync activity for 6 consecutive months will be automatically deleted, along with all associated personal data. Upon deletion, your data will be removed in accordance with the erasure process described above. Please ensure you use the App regularly to avoid automatic deletion of your account.

5. Marketing

We do not use your personal data for direct marketing. If this changes in the future, we will first obtain your explicit opt-in consent and provide you with a clear way to opt out at any time.

6. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of it, as required by GDPR Article 33. Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to you personally, we will also notify you directly without undue delay.

7. What are your data protection rights?

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we erase your personal data. This can be done at any time by you directly through the account deletion function in the App.
  • The right to restrict processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • The right to data portability: You can request that we transfer your data to another organization or directly to you in a machine-readable format, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You can object to our processing of your personal data.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond. Please contact us using the details at the end of this policy to exercise any of these rights.

8. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites and applications to store information. In our application, we use cookies specifically to maintain your secure login session, allowing you to stay signed in between app uses.

9. How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for a single, essential purpose:

  • Authentication: To keep you securely signed in to your account across sessions.

10. What types of cookies do we use?

We use only strictly necessary (functional) cookies. These cookies are essential for the core service you request (a persistent, secure login) and do not serve any marketing, analytics, or tracking purposes. Without them, the feature of remaining logged in would not work.

11. How to manage cookies

As these cookies are essential for a core function of the App, they cannot be disabled without breaking the login feature. However, you can effectively clear these cookies at any time by logging out of the App through your account settings. This action will remove the session cookie and require you to log in again on next use.

12. Privacy policies of other websites

Our App may contain links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to our App.

13. Changes to our privacy policy

We keep our privacy policy under regular review and will place any updates on this page or within the App.

14. How to contact us

If you have questions, wish to exercise your data rights, or have concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us via our Discord community or by opening an issue on GitHub.
